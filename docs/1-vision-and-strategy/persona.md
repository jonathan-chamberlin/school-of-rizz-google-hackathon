# Ideal Client Avatar / Persona Doc

## Demographics
- Male, neurodivergent, intelligent, socially awkward.
- College student or early career professional.
- Living in a city where meeting people is easier, or someone with a few friends already where they can test new behaviors.

## Psychological Traits
- Analytical thinker — responds to structure, measurement, and clear incremental wins.
- Wants practical, repeatable training; dislikes therapy language and fluff.
- Performance-oriented mindset.

## Pain Points
- Feels isolated; tries to connect but gets rejected and doesn't understand why.
- Loneliness, depression, anxiety, and hopelessness from repeated social failure.

## What He's Already Tried
- Watched YouTube videos about social skills — only minimal changes resulted.
- Tried cold approaching more people — social anxiety and a lack of results makes it feel impossible.

## Limiting Beliefs
- Believes he is cursed with being more analytical and less social.
- In reality, being more analytical is a huge gift because it helps him learn new things faster than people who are more naturally social. You can learn to be social, but they can't learn to be analytical.

## Objections / Skepticism About Roomcraft
- Maybe the roleplays don't actually apply to situations he'd find himself in in the real world.
- An AI voice agent might not accurately simulate the way a conversation might actually happen, with its unpredictability.
- The voice agent might not be able to identify his tone accurately.
