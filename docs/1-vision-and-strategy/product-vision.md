# Product Vision Doc

## Problem
Neurodivergent people feel isolated because people don't vibe with them. They try to connect but often get rejected and don't understand why. This leads to loneliness, depression, anxiety, and a feeling of hopelessness.

## Solution
Roomcraft. This app helps people create relationships by giving you the skills to connect. Imagine that whenever you spoke, people laughed. Imagine that people invite you to hangouts because you're so enjoyable to be around. Imagine whenever you spoke to someone you were attracted to, they ask you for your number. How would that help you? How much happier would you be? It helps with deepening existing relationships with friends, family, and coworkers. It also helps you make new friends.

## Differentiator
Every other option (YouTube channels, books, dating coaches) gives you information — but learning only happens upon doing. Roomcraft's voice agent helps you actually improve your skillset and change your behavior instead of just making you feel good for reading text or watching a video you'll never apply. Most YouTube channels tell you what to do but don't give feedback on the way you actually communicate, so you can't tell how relevant the advice is to you. Roomcraft closes that gap with live, personalized feedback on your actual delivery.

## What Roomcraft Is NOT
- It is not therapy.
- It is not a feel-good doomscroll app.
- It is not about pickup.
- It's about improving your communication, social skills, and relationship skills.

## Identity Progression
Beginner → Conversationalist → Connector → Room Leader

## Transformation Promise
Frame charisma as a learnable craft: study → rehearse → apply → reflect. Move the user toward being "a conversationalist with an above-average social life."
