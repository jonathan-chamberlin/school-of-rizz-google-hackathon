# Product Vision Doc

## Problem
Most guys know they could be better socially — with girls, with friends, in groups — but there's no way to actually practice. You can watch Hamza videos all day, but when the moment comes, you freeze. Information doesn't change behavior. Reps do.

## Solution
School of Rizz. Open the app, pick a scenario, and talk your way through it — on camera, with live AI feedback. You get a rizz score at the end. Share it, screenshot it, flex on your friends, or learn from it.

The core loop:
1. Pick a scenario (realistic or absurd)
2. Talk through it on camera with the AI character
3. Get live coaching feedback as you go (on by default)
4. After ~3 minutes, get your rizz score + breakdown (warmth, confidence, wit, etc.)
5. Share the screenshot or video

## The Hook
"Get your rizz score."

One free roleplay with coaching. After ~3 minutes you get your score and the Umax-style results screenshot. Detailed feedback and unlimited sessions behind the paywall.

## Differentiator
Every other option gives you information. School of Rizz gives you reps. It's a personal rizz coach that actually listens to you talk, watches your face, and tells you what's working and what's not — in real time.
