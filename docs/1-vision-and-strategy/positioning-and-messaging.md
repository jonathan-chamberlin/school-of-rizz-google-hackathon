# Positioning & Messaging Doc

## Category Definition
Social skill training app with AI-powered practice and live feedback.

## What Roomcraft Is NOT
- Not therapy.
- Not a feel-good doomscroll app.
- Not pickup artist content.
- Not another "watch and forget" content library.

## Core Messaging Pillars
1. Learning only happens upon doing — not reading, not watching.
2. Live, personalized feedback on how you actually communicate.
3. Charisma is a learnable craft, not a fixed trait.
4. Being analytical is a gift — you learn faster than naturally social people.

## Tone of Voice
Disciplined, performance-oriented, analytical, slightly premium.

## Language to Avoid
- Therapy language ("healing journey", "safe space", "vulnerability")
- Gamey/childish language ("you leveled up!", "streak fire!")
- Pickup/seduction framing
- Vague motivational fluff ("you got this!", "believe in yourself")

## Emotional Promise
You're not broken — you just never had a system. Roomcraft gives you the reps, the feedback, and the structure to become someone people want around.
