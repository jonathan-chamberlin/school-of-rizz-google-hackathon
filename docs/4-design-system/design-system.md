## Microcopy Guidelines
- Tone: direct, instructional, slightly elevated.
- Avoid therapy language ("healing journey", "safe space").
- Avoid fluffy praise. Praise should be specific.
- Avoid gamey language ("you leveled up!"). XP and levels exist but copy stays professional.
- Keep CTAs action-oriented: "Practice Now", "Repeat", "Log Situation", "Replay Attempt".
- Use clear objectives and short microtasks (3-8 minutes typical).
